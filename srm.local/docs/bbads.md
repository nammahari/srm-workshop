## About the Program

**BBA (Honours)** is a three-year undergraduate program designed for students aspiring to build an intensive career in management. The program provides both academic rigor and industrial exposure, making students industry-ready.

The curriculum is structured to impart:
- Industry-specific knowledge  
- Strong academic foundations  
- Practical exposure through internships  

Students are encouraged to specialize in a focused domain, enabling them to manage, start, and grow new or existing businesses effectively.

### Specializations Offered
- Marketing  
- Finance  
- Human Resources  
- Operations Management  

A **one-month internship** in the field of interest enhances domain expertise and employability.
