## Program Focus

The program is structured to develop:

- Strong foundations in computer science and mathematics  
- Secure system and software design principles  
- Deep understanding of networks and operating systems  
- Applied cybersecurity, risk management, and professional ethics  

The curriculum balances **theoretical rigor**, **hands-on laboratories**, and **industry-aligned security practices**.
