    Campus Facilities

    Academic Facilities

    State-of-the-Art Laboratories

    The EEE department is equipped with modern laboratories including:

    • Power Systems Laboratory
    • Control Systems Laboratory
    • Electrical Machines Laboratory
    • Power Electronics Laboratory
    • Microcontrollers & Embedded Systems Lab
    • Digital Systems Laboratory
    • Analog Electronics Laboratory
    • Measurements & Instrumentation Lab
    • High Voltage Engineering Lab

    Central Research Facilities

    • IIISM (Institute of Innovation in Science and Management)
    • REACH (Research and Advanced Center for Health)
    • NRC (Nanotechnology Research Centre)
    • High-Performance Computing Center (HPCC)

    Library Resources

    • Central Library with extensive collection of:
    • Technical books and journals
    • E-books and digital resources
    • IEEE, Springer, Elsevier subscriptions
    • Research papers and conference proceedings
    • Digital Library access 24/7
    • Reading Halls for quiet study

    Placement Cell

    The Career Centre at SRMIST provides: - Pre-placement Training: Aptitude, technical skills, soft skills - Industry Internships: Summer and semester-long programs - Campus Recruitment: Top companies visit for placements - Career Counseling: One-on-one guidance - Alumni Network: Mentorship and networking

    • Nearest Airport: Chennai International Airport (30 km)
    • Taxi/Cab: Available from airport

    • Nearest Railway Station: Chengalpattu Junction (8 km)
